Getting Started

Welcome to the Smart Attendance System. Here's how to use the system:

Student Registration

  • Go to Upload Students section
  • Upload Excel file with student information
  • Ensure all required fields are filled

Taking Attendance

  • Navigate to Take Attendance section
  • Click "Start Attendance"
  • Follow the on-screen instructions

Viewing Attendance

  • Go to Attendance Details section
  • Use filters to view specific records
  • Export reports as needed
